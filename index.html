Cold coffee

Blend chilled milk, coffee, sugar, and ice until frothy, then pour into a glass and top with ice cream or whipped cream for a rich café-style cold coffee.



Receipe

Chocolate coffee

For a quick chocolate coffee, brew 1 cup of hot coffee, stir in 1 tablespoon cocoa powder and 1–2 teaspoons sugar, then add ½ cup warm milk. Mix well, top with whipped cream or chocolate shavings, and serve hot.



Receipe

Milk Tea

To make milk tea, boil 1 cup of water and add 1–2 teaspoons of tea leaves (or a tea bag). Let it simmer for 2–3 minutes, then add ½ cup milk and sugar to taste. Boil for another minute, strain into a cup, and enjoy hot.
